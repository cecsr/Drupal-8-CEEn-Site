Software Access

The college provides Citrix and RGS application servers. There are useful programs on citrix such as Word and Excel under "Office Programs" and MathCAD under "Math Programs." Information on those servers can be found at http://caedm.et.byu.edu under the "Remote Computing" tab.

CB 234 Software

The Civil Engineering department provides a computer lab in CB 234. Due to classes being scheduled in that room our software is also available in the CAEDM computer labs.

Free Software

The department provides all the software you may need on our computer labs. Much of our software is extremely expensive and would be out of the financial reach of a student. However a few companies have academic licensing programs that allow students to get their software for free.

AutoCAD, Revit, and Civil 3D can be obtained by going to http://students.autodesk.com.

Microsoft software (NOT including Microsoft Office) can be obtained via the CAEDM MSDNAA account which can be found at http://caedm.et.byu.edu under the "Labs and Software" tab at the Free Microsoft Downloads link.  

VMWare software can also be obtained at http://caedm.et.byu.edu under the "Labs and Software" tab.
