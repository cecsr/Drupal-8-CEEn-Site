Tensile Testers (CB 123)

MTS - Tensile Tester (CB 123):

  • Tensile tester for tensile or compressive loads.
  • Load Capacity: 110,000 lb.
  • Resolution: 10 lb.
  • Length of Stroke: 6" (-3" to 3")
  • Special Training Required
  • If you would like to be trained on this machine, schedule BOTH the instructor (use links for ME Staff above) and the machine (using the link below) for about an hour.

Instron - Tensile Tester (CB 123):

  • Tensile tester for tensile, compressive, or torsional loads.
  • Load Capacity: 20,000 lb.
  • Resolution: 2 lb.
  • Length of Stroke: 4" (-2" to 2")
  • Special Training Required
  • If you would like to be trained on this machine, schedule BOTH the instructor (use links for ME Staff above) and the machine (using the link below) for about an hour.
